# BDM Gym Revenue & Member Retention Analysis

This repository contains a Business Data Management (BDM) capstone project focused on analyzing revenue stability and member retention for **Muscle & Fitness 2 Gym**, a small-scale fitness center in Agartala, Tripura.

The project converts manually maintained gym payment records into a structured Excel-based analysis system to identify revenue trends, membership churn risk, customer segments, and actionable business recommendations.

---

## Project Objective

The main objective of this project was to help the gym owner improve business decision-making by analyzing:

- Monthly revenue and profit trends
- Membership renewal behavior
- Active, at-risk, and churn-risk members
- Customer segments using RFM analysis
- Business impact of improved tracking and retention strategies

---

## Business Problem

The gym maintained member registration and payment records manually in physical registers. This created challenges such as:

- Difficulty tracking membership expiry dates
- Lack of visibility into active and expired members
- Inconsistent membership renewals
- Revenue fluctuations across months
- Limited data-driven decision-making

---

## Data Source

The project uses **primary data** collected from the gym’s manual payment registers.

- Data period: **March 2025 to August 2025**
- Tool used: **Microsoft Excel**
- Analysis date: **31 August 2025**

The dataset includes member details, payment dates, plan types, service types, payment amounts, membership expiry dates, and payment methods.

> Note: Sensitive member information should be anonymized before public sharing.

## Tools & Techniques Used

- Microsoft Excel
- Data cleaning and preprocessing
- Excel formulas and calculated columns
- Pivot Tables
- Pivot Charts
- Dashboard creation
- Membership lifecycle analysis
- RFM customer segmentation

---

## Methodology

The project followed these steps:

1. Collected primary data from manual gym registers
2. Digitized and cleaned the data in Excel
3. Standardized date formats and removed inconsistencies
4. Created calculated fields such as expiry date and membership status
5. Classified members into Active, At Risk, and Churn Risk groups
6. Performed monthly revenue and profit analysis
7. Applied RFM analysis using Recency, Frequency, and Monetary values
8. Built an Excel dashboard using Pivot Tables and Charts
9. Interpreted findings and developed business recommendations

---

## Key Findings

- Monthly revenue showed significant fluctuations.
- Profitability was highly dependent on consistent membership renewals.
- A large proportion of members were classified as churn risk.
- RFM analysis showed a limited loyal customer base.
- Manual tracking created gaps in renewal monitoring and revenue visibility.

---

## Recommendations

Based on the analysis, the following recommendations were proposed:

- Implement a structured digital membership tracking system
- Monitor expiry dates and send renewal reminders
- Focus on retaining at-risk and churn-risk members
- Use dashboard monitoring for monthly business decisions
- Promote personal training services to improve revenue per member

---

## Expected Business Impact

The proposed solution can help the gym achieve:

- Improved revenue stability
- Better membership renewal tracking
- Reduced churn risk
- Higher customer retention
- Improved operational efficiency
- Better data-driven decision-making
